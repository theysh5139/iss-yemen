### 1. How do I register for an event? (Priority: 10)
**Response:**
"To register for an event, you need to be a member first. Go to the 'All Events' page, find the event you want to join, and click the 'Register' button. Fill in your details (name, email, matric number, phone), upload payment proof if required, and submit the registration form."

**Related Keywords:** register event, join event, event registration, sign up event, event signup

### 2. How do I become a member of the ISS Yemen club? (Priority: 9)

### 8. How do I pay for event registration? (Priority: 8)
**Response:**
"When registering for a paid event, you'll see a QR code and account number on the registration form. You can scan the QR code or transfer the registration fee to the provided account number. After payment, upload your transaction proof (PDF or image) in the registration form before submitting."

**Related Keywords:** payment, pay event, event fee, registration fee, payment method, how to pay
